We handle all types of Property Mutation & Ownership Transfer cases in Ghaziabad & Indirapuram. Mutation is required after property purchase, gift, inheritance, sale deed, will, or partition. Our services ensure fast processing and legally clean records in Nagar Nigam, Revenue Department, and Tehsil.

✔ What We Do

  • Mutation after Sale Deed
  • Gift Deed / Will Mutation
  • Inheritance & Succession Transfer
  • Property Tax Name Change in Nagar Nigam
  • Khata Transfer in Revenue Records
  • Correction of old entries / mismatched details
  • Verification of Seller / Previous Owner Records
  • Assistance in Tehsil, Registry Office & Municipal Office

✔ Required Documents

  • Registered Sale Deed / Gift Deed / Will
  • ID Proof & Address Proof
  • Chain of Ownership Papers
  • Latest Property Tax Receipt
  • Mutation Application & Affidavit

✔ Why Choose Us

We ensure quick processing, correct documentation, and error-free entries. With experience in Tehsil & Nagar Nigam procedures, we make mutation smooth, transparent, and legally secure.
